Who I Am

Medical student. Student of the Quran.

Matched into psychiatry, starting residency this July. Working on memorizing the Quran alongside that. The thing I keep coming back to is what al-Ghazali and Ibn Qayyim were describing — and how much of it maps onto what psychiatry is only recently putting language to.

Not a scholar. Mostly writing for Muslim premeds and MSA students who are thinking about the same stuff.
